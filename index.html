What is Banji?

Banji Japanese Cuisine is located at 333 Atlantic City Blvd in Bayville, New Jersey.
Banji serves hearty portions of simple, delicious japanese food. Diners can enjoy a stimulating choice of traditional and hybrid japanese dishes, including plenty of option for soup, salad, appetizers, sushi, sashimi, rolls, entrees from the kitchen and sushi bar with amazing value and unbeatable prices.
